# Business Problem

## Overview

Senior leaders rely on timely, accurate, and trusted workforce data to make strategic decisions regarding staffing, manpower allocation, workforce readiness, recruiting, and retention.

When workforce data is spread across multiple systems or lacks consistency, leaders may struggle to identify staffing gaps, monitor trends, and make informed operational decisions.

## Objective

Design a workforce analytics framework that transforms enterprise personnel data into actionable insights through data validation, integration, visualization, and executive reporting.

## Leadership Questions

- Do we have enough personnel to accomplish the mission?
- Which units have the largest staffing gaps?
- Where are retention concerns emerging?
- Are manpower allocations aligned with operational needs?
- Which workforce trends require leadership attention?

## Business Value

Provide leadership with trusted data that supports informed, timely, and strategic workforce decisions.
